All of the resources below are ones we've used or have come highly recommended from friends who have landed top venture capital offers. These resources will help you succeed in the venture capital interview process.

Groups

Places you can go to connect and collaborate with other (aspiring) venture capitalists.

Emerging Venture Capitalists Association is a community where many students and professionals share resources related to venture capital. You can find potential collaborators by making a post or joining one of the many message groups on the site.

Venture Capital Resource Database

We've compiled a database of excellent books for you to explore relating to both finance and venture capital.

Linked here are a compilation of guides, books, and networking tools to get started with your career in venture capital. If there are any resources you think are missing mention it in the form attached here.

  • VC Blogs and Newsletters

    With an industry so focused on current market trends and gaps within those spaces, constant learning is essential. The blogs and newsletters linked below will help you stay up-to-date with the latest in venture capital.

    • Stratechery - Known for its insightful analysis on technology and business strategy, Stratechery provides a deep dive into trends shaping the VC landscape.

    • Paul Graham's Essays - Written by one of the co-founders of Y Combinator, these essays cover everything from startups to philosophy, offering timeless wisdom for aspiring investors and founders.

    • CB Insights - CB Insights offers data-driven insights and reports on emerging industries and venture trends, making it a go-to for understanding high-growth sectors and funding activity.

    • Axios Pro Rata - A concise newsletter covering daily VC and private equity news, Axios Pro Rata is perfect for staying informed about major deals and market movements.

  • Financial Modeling Prep

    While financial modeling isn't as prominent in a largely sourcing-focused venture capital role, understanding the technicals behind is still important. Linked is a template to learn about Cap Tables, and here is a brief introduction to VC Modeling.

  • VC Deals Course

    In addition to the resources above, Techstars and Kauffman Fellows have partnered to create a VC Deals Course that covers the basics of venture capital investing, including deal sourcing, due diligence, and negotiation.
